Biography
I'm pursuing my M.S. in Computer Science at USC, advised by Prof. Yue Wang and Prof. Daniel Seita. Previously, I finished my B.S. in Computer Science at UC San Diego, advised by Prof. Xiaolong Wang. My research interests include robot learning and computer vision. Recently, i've worked on robot simulation for manipulation and locomotion.
